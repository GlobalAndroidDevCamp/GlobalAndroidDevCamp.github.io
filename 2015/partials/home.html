Global Android Dev Camp

GDG hackathon series for 2015

Finals of Android Study Jam and Virtual Spaces

Back in 2012, the global Android developer community worked together to create an Android hackathon that took place at over 50 cities world wide. This was a amazing! Now, we are in 2015, Android has changed since, everything got more professional and there we go:

The Global Android Dev Camp takes place on
20-22 March 2015.

The global Android Study Jam takes places in February and March. The main themes:

  • Connected Spaces and
  • Virtual Spaces.

During that weekend developers and designers are invited to sit together and to

  • Develop apps for based on their knowledge gathered during the Android Study jam
  • Create, try and improve tools and libraries around virtual spaces like cardboard, vuforia,...
  • Create, try and improve Android apps around connected spaces like wearables, dev boards, iot,...
